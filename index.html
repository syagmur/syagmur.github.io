Welcome to my website!


I received my BS degrees from Istanbul Technical University in Mathematical Engineering and Electronics Engineering with a double major program. I also received my MS degree from the Computer Science and Engineering Department at Sabanci University. After I completed my PhD in the Cognitive Systems Lab at Northeastern University under the supervision of Deniz Erdogmus in 2020, I joined Philips Ultrasound Inc. as Systems Design Engineer and developed algorithms for the GPU signal path. Currently, I am a Sr. Applied AI/ML Scientist at Inari Medical Inc., where I develop learning-based solutions for biomedical images. My current research interests include image quality assessment and biomedical signal processing.
